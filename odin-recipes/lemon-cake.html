Lemon Cake

Lemon PoppySeed Bundt Cake

Description

We all love the flavor of lemon and poppy seed, right? This recipe will fill those cravings with moist, fluffy cake and a sweet and fruity icing drizzle. Inject the cake with fruit filling or cream cheese to take it to the next level!

Ingredients

  • 1(15.25 oz) package lemon cake mix
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cuup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1(3 oz) package instant lemon pudding mix
  • 1 lemon (zested)
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds (Optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon extract

Directions

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a fluted tube cake pan.
  2. Step 2: Beat cake mix, sour cream, eggs, vegetable oil, lemon juice, pudding mix, lemon zest, poppy seeds, and lemon extract together in a bowl with an electric mixer on low speed for 1 minute. Increase speed to medium; beat for 3 minutes. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 45 minutes. Cool in the pan on a wire rack for 10 minutes. Tap the pan firmly a few times, then shake it gently to loosen the cake. Place the wire rack over the pan and invert to release cake onto the rack; cool completely.
